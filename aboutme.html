USER EXPERIENCE

the overall experience of a person using a product such as a websuite or computer application, especially inh terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use.

Poor design, structurally or aesthetically, will greatly effect user experience.

The infamous "norman door" comes to mind whenever I think of poor design and poor user experience (UX). I came to understand the basics of UX while interning for an AR/VR start-up. With AR/VR's limitations, the greatest challenge was creating controls in a 360 degree immserve environment, without disrupting the experience. With observation and research, the team and I were able to find ways to improve user experience by implementing intuitive controls, all the while maintaining the immersive experience. These types of lessons have stuck with me and I methodologically think about it when creating a product.
